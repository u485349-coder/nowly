Built for spontaneity, not scheduling theater.

The point of Nowly is simple: reduce the friction between “anyone free?” and actually linking up. No bloated event flow. No planning burden. Just live availability, smart overlap, and fast social motion.

Signal availability

Free now, free later, busy, down this weekend. Add a vibe and radius, then let it expire on its own.

Detect overlap

Nowly watches for friend overlap using time, distance, responsiveness, and lightweight social context.

Turn it into a hangout

Send a quick proposal, start a group thread, share ETA, and keep the social loop moving.

Why it feels different

Nowly is designed to feel alive. It favors low input, notification-timed momentum, and real-world friend density over heavy profiles or long planning rituals.

Low-pressure by default

Quick bite, coffee run, walking nearby, pull up for a bit. The product lowers the social burden instead of adding it.

Network effects that matter

Import your people, invite your crew, and make one campus or neighborhood feel dense before you scale wider.

Social rhythm, not isolated events

Nowly reinforces momentum with recaps, streaks, overlap nudges, and signs that your people are active right now.

Real backend, real-time core

Expo mobile app, Express API, Prisma/Postgres, Socket.io threads, Twilio-ready OTP, and Railway-friendly deployment.
