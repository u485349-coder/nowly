Built for spontaneity, not scheduling theater.

The point of Nowly is simple: reduce the friction between "anyone free?" and actually linking up. No bloated event flow. No planning burden. Just live availability, smart overlap, recurring booking links, and fast social motion across phone and browser.

Signal availability

Free now, free later, busy, down this weekend. Add a vibe, custom mode, and live duration, then let it expire on its own.

Detect overlap and future fit

Nowly watches for live friend overlap and recurring schedule fit using time, distance, responsiveness, and lightweight social context.

Turn it into a hangout

Send a quick proposal, share a booking link, open a private chat, or spin up a group thread fast.

Why it feels different

Nowly is designed to feel alive. It favors low input, notification-timed momentum, and real-world friend density over heavy profiles or long planning rituals.

Low-pressure by default

Quick bite, coffee run, walking nearby, pull up for a bit, or a custom prompt in your own words. The product lowers the social burden instead of adding it.

Network effects that matter

Import your people, invite your crew, and make one campus or neighborhood feel dense before you scale wider.

Social rhythm, not isolated events

Nowly reinforces momentum with recaps, recurring availability, overlap nudges, and signs that your people are active right now.

Real app on phone and browser

One Expo Router codebase powers mobile and browser, while the landing site acts like a launcher that can log you in or open Nowly fast.

Use Nowly on your phone or in your browser.

The browser app follows the same core product flow as mobile: phone login, live radar, recurring availability, shareable booking links, private chats, group chats, proposals, and recap loops. The landing page now acts as a clean launcher for both.
